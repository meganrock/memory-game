MEMORY

To start the game, select difficulty level and click the New Game button. Click on cards to flip them over. Flip over two cards at a time to see if they’re a match. If they’re not a match, they will automatically flip back. Try to remember where you saw images before so you can match them faster later. Make as many matches as you can before time runs out. Good luck!
